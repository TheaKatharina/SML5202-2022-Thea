Curriculum Vitae

What is a Curriculum Vitae (CV)?

A CV is a document listing your academic and professional qualifications, experience and accomplishments. CV stands for the Latin phrase ‘curriculum vitae’ which means ‘course of life.’ (Source: www.resume.com )

Learning objectives

On the following pages you will learn how to write a CV.

Firstly, do the exercise vocabulary about CV-writing .

Secondly,take a look at the CV-template and write your own CV!
