Job Interview

Watch the first part of the film (0:00-03:18)

Watch the first part of the film a second time and take notes:

  1. What is the employer's first impression of Leo?
  2. How does Leo excuse his delay?
  3. Do you think that Leo's clothing is appropiate? Why?
  4. Do you think Leo's behaviour is impolite? Why?
  5. How could Leo improve?

Then think about the following questions. How would you have answered them?

  1. Can you introduce yourself?
  2. What is your greatest weakness?
  3. Why did you choose our company?
  4. Can you tell the reasons why you should be hired?
