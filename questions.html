Strengths and weaknesses

A frequently asked interview question is about your strengths and weaknesses. Take some time to think about them and make a new entry in the padlet! You could think of the following questions:

  • What am I good at?
  • How would others describe me?
  • What am I passionate about?
  • How can I improve? In doing what? Why?
